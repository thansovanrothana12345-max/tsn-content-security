# User Manual - TSN Copyright Defender

This manual helps analysts and copyright reviewers navigate, scan, and manage copyright infringement timelines on the platform.

---

## 1. Landing Dashboard
- **Case Statistics**: Displays counters for critical, high, medium, and low priority folders.
- **Leak Map**: Shows resolution progress charts of active verification workflows.
- **Sidebar Navigation**: Includes access links to:
  - **Dashboard**: High-level metrics summaries.
  - **Case Manager**: Core case folders workspace.
  - **Scan Center**: URL scanning input.
  - **Evidence Ledger**: Matching results database.

---

## 2. Managing Cases
1. **Create Case**:
   - Click the "New Case" button in Case Manager.
   - Enter a title, description, priority (Low, Medium, High, Critical), client name, and target platform tags.
2. **Case Timeline**:
   - Click on a Case Card to open the timeline window.
   - Chronological historical updates show notes, uploads, and edit logs.
3. **Annotations & Notes**:
   - Write custom notes at the bottom of the timeline view to log legal reviews or analyst comments.
4. **Archive & Export**:
   - Click "Archive Case" to transition status to Archived.
   - Click "Export Case Package" to download a ZIP archive containing all case notes, evidence details, and PDF takedown notice reports.

---

## 3. URL Scanning (Scan Center)
1. Navigate to **Scan Center**.
2. Paste the target infringing URL.
   - *Supported Platforms*: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook (standard posts and Watch links), Facebook Ad Library, and generic web pages.
3. Select an optional target **Case Folder** to attach findings immediately.
4. Click **Start Scan Job**.
   - The job is queued in the background. Analysts can monitor progress (Pending -> Running -> Completed/Failed) from the status card.

---

## 4. Evidence Resolution & Reports
1. **Analyze Results**:
   - Browse the **Evidence Ledger** to view screenshots, metadata, and timestamps of detected leaks.
2. **Notice Compilation**:
   - Select the Case and click **Generate Notice**.
   - Choose a target legal template (YouTube, Meta, TikTok, standard notice, or Cease & Desist letters).
   - Sign inside the canvas panel signature pad and generate the PDF or Word DOCX takedown package.
